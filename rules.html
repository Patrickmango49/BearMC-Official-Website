Rules

Please follow these rules to keep the server fun and safe for everyone.

  • 1. No racism of any kind and for any reason
  • 2. No sharing public information unless you have permission
  • 3. Do not be disrespectful to any mod or player
  • 4. No impersonating staff or players
  • 5. No scamming of any type (unless on anarchy)
  • 6. No sharing accounts. You are responsible for your own account.
  • 7. No hacking accounts. Logging into any Minecraft account without permission may result in a ban (6 months to permanent).
  • 8. No duplication exploits. IP bans may be applied depending on severity.
  • 9. No radar/mini map mods of any kind. Waypoint mods are allowed.
  • 10. No hacks, including but not limited to:
    • X-Ray
    • Kill Aura
    • Fly Hack
    • Speed Hack
    • Jesus Hack
    • NoFall
    • Auto Clicker
    • Fast Click
    • ESP (Player ESP, Chest ESP)
    • Phase / NoClip
    • Freecam
    • Inventory Hacks
    • Duplication Glitches
    • Crash Exploits
  • 11. No spamming the chat
